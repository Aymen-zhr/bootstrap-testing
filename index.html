The Elden Ring

The Golden Order has been broken. Rise, Tarnished, and be guided by grace to brandish the Elden Ring and become the Elden Lord in the Lands Between. In the Lands Between ruled by Queen Marika the Eternal, the Elden Ring, the source of the Erdtree, has been shattered. Soon, Marika's offspring, demigods all, claimed the shards of the Elden Ring known as the Great Runes, and the mad taint of their newfound strength triggered a war: The Shattering.
